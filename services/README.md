## [Azure Well Architected Framework](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/well-architected/)

The Five Pillars to the Well Architected Framework

* P - Performance - Adjust to changes in demands placed on the workload by through horizontal scaling and testing changes before deploying to production.
* R - Reliability - Ensures that the workload meets the uptime and recovery targets by building redundancy and resiliency at scale.
* O - Operational Excellence - Reduce issues in production by building holistic observability and automated systems.
* C - Cost Optimization - Adopt an optimization mindset by at organizational, architectural, and tactical levels to keep your spending within budget.
* S - Security - Protect the workload from attacks by maintaining confidentiality and data integrity.
